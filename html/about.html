About Us

Hi! We're Joshua and Isaac — the brothers behind Cabinet Bros.

Joshua's woodworking journey began at just 4 years old, building birdhouses with his grandpa. That early spark grew into a lifelong passion. With the help of his dad and hands-on classes, Joshua has continued sharpening his skills and now focuses on building quality, handcrafted cabinets that are built to last. Now 20 years old, he's proud to turn a childhood love into a business he truly believes in

Isaac, the younger brother, started programming at age 10 and quickly discovered a love for tech and design. Over the years, he’s learned several programming languages and developed a sharp eye for layout and aesthetics. At 18, Isaac brings that creative and technical expertise to help design the perfect kitchen spaces for clients.

Both Joshua and Isaac were homeschooled — Joshua since 3rd grade and Isaac since 1st — and they’re grateful for the freedom it gave them to grow in the areas they love. At Cabinet Bros, they combine craftsmanship and creativity to deliver beautiful, functional cabinets for your home.

Let us help bring your vision to life — one cabinet at a time.
